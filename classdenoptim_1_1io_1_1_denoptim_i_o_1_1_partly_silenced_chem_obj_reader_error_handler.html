Detailed Description

This differs from DefaultChemObjectReaderErrorHandler just by ignoring messages about invalid symbol.

However, to achieve this result we need to overcome the closed permission mask of DefaultChemObjectReaderErrorHandler where the logger is private. As a result, we have to duplicate nearly the entire class.
